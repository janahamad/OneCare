# OneCare User Guide

Welcome to **OneCare**, the ultimate platform for beauty and wellness bookings. This guide explains how to use the platform based on your role.

---

## 1. Administrators (Admin)
The Admin panel is the central hub for managing the entire platform.

*   **Access**: Go to `https://onecare.jxtechstudio.com/admin`
*   **Key Features**:
    *   **Manage Users**: Oversee all registered users, vendors, and admins.
    *   **System Configuration**: Adjust global settings, categories, and locations.
    *   **High-level Overview**: Monitor all salons, bookings, and platform activity.

---

## 2. Service Providers (Vendors/Salons)
Service Providers use a dedicated panel to manage their business operations.

*   **Access**: Go to `https://onecare.jxtechstudio.com/vendor`
*   **Registration**: Salon owners can register their business via the "Register Your Business" link on the landing page.
*   **Key Features**:
    *   **Salon Profile**: Manage your salon's name, logo, location, and description.
    *   **Service Management**: Add, edit, or remove services (e.g., Hair Artistry, Skin Renaissance) with pricing and duration.
    *   **Business Hours**: Set and update your opening and closing times.
    *   **Booking Management**: View and manage appointments requested by users.
    *   **Gallery**: Upload photos of your space to showcase to potential clients.

---

## 3. Regular Users (Clients)
Clients use the frontend website to discover and book services.

*   **Access**: Visit `https://onecare.jxtechstudio.com/`
*   **Key Steps**:
    1.  **Exploration**: Browse featured services like "Hair Artistry" and "Skin Renaissance" on the landing page.
    2.  **Language Toggle**: Switch between **English** and **Arabic** using the link in the top navigation bar.
    3.  **Account Creation**: Sign up or log in via the "Sign In" / "Sign Up" buttons in the header.
    4.  **Booking a Service**:
        *   Navigate to a Salon's profile.
        *   Browse their **Services Menu**.
        *   Click **"Select"** on your desired service.
        *   Click **"Book Now"** to open the booking interface.
        *   *(Note: Detailed booking scheduling is currently being polished and will display as "Coming Soon" in some areas).*
    5.  **Logout**: Manage your session through the user menu in the header.

---

## Technical Note
The platform is secured with **HTTPS** to ensure all your data and transactions are encrypted.
